Primary Source Liability Verification

Website that acts as a clearinghouse for liability carriers, extracting liability coverage and claims history, for credentialing purposes
Removes the need for insurance carriers to fulfill verification of insurance requests, by automating the verification process
Reduces expenses for carrier by eliminating employee(s) time and dedication in responding to verification requests
Secure, compliant and removes HIPAA concerns, by utilizing appropriate file transfer protocols, instead of email to deliver policy and claims history
No cost to insurance carriers or providers
